Sticks

This is a hand game for two players.

Click first on one of your hands, then one of your opponent's to add an amount to their hand.

Finger addition is modulo 5. The aim is to reduce both opponent hands to zero.

The computer uses a variation of the minimax algorithm to select a move (Negamax)


Depth: This is the number of moves the computer will 'look ahead' in its search

Speed: The delay between moves/animation

Restart round: Maintains scores but restarts current play.

Reset game: Resets scores as well as the current play.
